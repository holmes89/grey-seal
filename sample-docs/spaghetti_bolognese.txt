Title: Spaghetti Bolognese
Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can crushed tomatoes
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried basil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 12 oz spaghetti
- Olive oil

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a pan. Saut√© onion and garlic until soft.
2. Add ground beef, cook until browned.
3. Stir in tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, and herbs. Simmer 20 minutes.
4. Boil spaghetti according to package directions.
5. Serve sauce over spaghetti.
