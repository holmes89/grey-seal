Title: Classic Chili
Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can kidney beans, drained
- 1 can diced tomatoes
- 2 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Brown ground beef in a pot, drain excess fat.
2. Add onion and garlic, cook until soft.
3. Stir in beans, tomatoes, and spices.
4. Simmer 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
