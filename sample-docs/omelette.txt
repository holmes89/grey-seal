Title: Cheese Omelette
Ingredients:
- 3 eggs
- 2 tbsp milk
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1/4 cup shredded cheese
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Beat eggs, milk, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
2. Heat butter in a nonstick pan, pour in egg mixture.
3. Cook gently until eggs begin to set, sprinkle cheese on one half.
