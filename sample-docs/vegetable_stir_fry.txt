Title: Vegetable Stir Fry
Ingredients:
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2 bell peppers, sliced
- 1 broccoli crown, cut into florets
- 2 carrots, julienned
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil

Instructions:
1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok over medium-high heat.
2. Stir-fry onion, garlic, and carrots for 2 minutes.
3. Add peppers and broccoli, stir-fry until crisp-tender.
4. Stir in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil.
5. Serve hot over rice or noodles.
