Title: Pancakes
Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 1/4 cups milk
- 1 egg
- 3 tbsp melted butter

Instructions:
1. In a bowl, mix dry ingredients.
2. Add milk, egg, and melted butter; whisk until smooth.
3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle, pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
4. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and brown the other side.
