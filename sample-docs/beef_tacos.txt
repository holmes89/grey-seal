Title: Beef Tacos
Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 packet taco seasoning
- 1/2 cup water
- Taco shells
- Toppings: lettuce, tomato, cheese, salsa, sour cream

Instructions:
1. Cook ground beef in a skillet until browned. Drain fat.
2. Stir in taco seasoning and water, simmer until thickened.
3. Fill taco shells with beef and toppings of choice.
