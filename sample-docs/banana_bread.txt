Title: Banana Bread
Ingredients:
- 2 to 3 ripe bananas
- 1/3 cup melted butter
- 1 tsp baking soda
- Pinch of salt
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 beaten egg
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 1/2 cups flour

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Mash bananas, stir in melted butter.
3. Mix in sugar, egg, and vanilla. Sprinkle baking soda and salt.
4. Add flour, mix well, pour into greased loaf pan.
5. Bake for 1 hour, cool before slicing.
