Title: Chicken Curry
Ingredients:
- 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
- 2 tbsp curry powder
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can coconut milk
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 2 tbsp oil
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a pot, saut√© onion, garlic, and ginger.
2. Add chicken, cook until browned.
3. Stir in curry powder, then coconut milk. Simmer 20 minutes.
4. Season and serve with rice.
