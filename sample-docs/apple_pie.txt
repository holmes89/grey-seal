Title: Apple Pie
Ingredients:
- 2 pie crusts
- 6 cups sliced apples
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp butter

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. Mix apples with sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice.
3. Fill crust with apple mixture, dot with butter.
4. Cover with top crust, cut slits for steam.
5. Bake 45–50 minutes until golden brown.
