Title: Lentil Soup
Ingredients:
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 cup lentils, rinsed
- 6 cups vegetable broth
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a pot, sauté onion, carrot, celery, and garlic until soft.
2. Stir in cumin, then lentils and broth.
3. Simmer for 30–40 minutes until lentils are tender.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
